
# 📘 Digital Literacy Project

## 📌 Introduction
Digital literacy refers to the ability to use digital technology, communication tools, and networks effectively. In today’s world, it is an essential skill for education, work, and daily life.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- To understand the concept of digital literacy  
- To learn basic computer and internet skills  
- To spread awareness about online safety and security  
- To understand the importance of digital tools in modern life  

---

## 📚 Topics Covered

### 1. Internet Basics
- What is the Internet  
- Uses of the Internet  
- Web browsers and search engines  

### 2. Digital Communication
- Email, social media, and messaging apps  
- Advantages and disadvantages  

### 3. Cyber Security
- Safe browsing practices  
- Password protection  
- Phishing and online scams  

### 4. Digital Payments
- Online banking  
- UPI and mobile wallets  
- Safety tips for transactions  

### 5. Importance of Digital Literacy
- Helps in education and learning  
- Improves job opportunities  
- Makes daily tasks easier
